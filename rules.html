About Us

Back to School is a quiz which focuses questions based on the KS2 curriculum. Back to School is a fun way children can test their knowledge, or teachers to audit their subject knoweldge. Parents can have a go to! Do you know more than your primary school child, or do they know more than you ?

How To Play

Back to School is a multiple choice quiz. Each correct question is worth 5 points. Each inccorect answer will minus 2 points so think carefully! Good Luck!
